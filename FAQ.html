WEDDING FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our wedding events.

Where is the Wedding venue located?

The wedding will be held at Sophia at the Prahran Arcade, located at 282 Chapel Street, Prahran.

Where is the Mehndi venue located?

The Mehndi will be held at Canvas House, located at 137 Buckhurst St, South Melbourne.

What time does the Wedding ceremony begin?

The ceremony will start at 4:30pm, we recommend arriving at least 30 minutes early.

What time does the Mehndi begin?

Please arrive to the venue at 6:00pm.

Are children able to attend?

We love your children, but due to space and venue restrictions we cannot accommodate little ones at our wedding events. We thank you for your understanding.

Gifts

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for! However, if you would like to contribute to our honeymoon, we will have a wishing well set up at our wedding reception.

Will parking be available for the Mehndi?

Street parking is available on Buckhurst Street. Please note, it is paid parking with a ticket required up until 6:00pm.

Will parking be available for the Wedding?

There are no available car parks onsite for use at the Prahran Arcade. Prahran Square is nearby and offers a multi-level car park that is recommended by the venue for parking.

Do the venues have accessibility access?

Please note, entry to the Prahran Arcade is via a staircase. If you require lift access, this is via the laneway off Princes Close. If you require a map to assist you in locating the lift, please let us know. The Mehndi venue is single story.

Do invitations include a plus one?

Due to the limited venue space we are only able to accommodate guests formally invited on your invitation. Thank you for your understanding!

Can guests take pictures and post to social media?

Yes! Please tag us and share your photos so we can see the events from your perspective.
