Mehndi

Thursday 3rd October 2024 - Evening Event

Dress Code: Traditional wear
We encourage you to wear traditional clothing to this wedding event, however this is not mandatory.
You can wear any colour you wish. There are no restrictions for this event.

Traditional wear recommendations:
Women:
Ghagra or Lehenga Choli
Shalwar Kameez
Saree
Anarkali

Men:
Kurta Pyjama
Sherwani

Where to Shop

There are many shops online you can buy traditional wear from. Stores include: Etsy, Indya, Kyna, even Amazon, eBay or Facebook Marketplace! If you would like to attend a physical store in Melbourne, the Little India district in Dandenong or Paaro in Glen Huntly are recommended.
You can choose clothing from wherever you like, we recommend that you read reviews and ensure you have your measurements before you make any purchases.


If you do not wish to wear traditional clothing, we encourage you to wear something bright and colourful to the Mehndi.
